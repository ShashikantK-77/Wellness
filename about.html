We are Wellness India.!

Wellness India is a leading carry and forwarding and consignee agent in the pharmaceutical industry, with over 20 years of experience in delivering high-quality products and services to clients across Maharashtra and pan India. We work with some of the top pharmaceutical companies, such as Cipla, Lakem, Alembic, Wallace, Abbott, and many more, providing reliable and innovative solutions to meet their needs.


Our expert team comprises experienced and dedicated professionals who are committed to delivering exceptional service and support to our clients. We have a robust infrastructure, advanced technology, and a customer-centric approach that enables us to provide timely and efficient delivery of pharmaceutical products at competitive rates.


At Wellness India, we believe in building strong relationships with our clients and partners, based on trust, respect, and mutual growth. We are committed to contributing to the betterment of society by delivering high-quality pharmaceutical products and services that meet the highest standards of safety and efficacy.
